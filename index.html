Our Mission

Consultants at the
intersection of capital
and clean technology.

As consultants focused on the small business space and the rapidly growing Climate Tech industry, our intent is to bridge the gap between climate-driven businesses seeking working capital and banks and other financiers interested in participating in the growing clean tech sector.

We are mission driven — committed to improving business access to the capital necessary for them to grow and prosper in challenging areas, and to scale ambitious technologies that can meaningfully improve quality of life for all. The Climate Tech sector is crucial for the future prosperity of our communities and our planet.

Featured Program

California IBank & Bay Area Air District

A revolutionary
partnership for
clean capital access.

We have worked with the California IBank loan guarantee program for many years. It is a powerful tool that assists interested lenders by reducing their lending risk — empowering climate entrepreneurs to secure the capital critical for their growth and impact.

The unique partnership between the California IBank and the Bay Area Air Quality Management District is revolutionary in its impact — opening new avenues of access to capital for Climate Technology businesses to improve community quality of life and create sustainable jobs.
